Welcome to

Maledath Events

Founded in 2014 in Perumbavoor, our event management company quickly established itself as a beacon of excellence. We are committed to delivering quality and personalized service for every event, limiting ourselves to a maximum of two events per day to ensure undivided attention and exceptional execution.

We treat every client like family. Bringing care, loyalty, and sincerity to each celebration is our promise. Our unique blend of traditional customs and modern aesthetics allows us to create events that honor your heritage while embracing contemporary styles.

Our chief event planner's hands-on presence at every event ensures meticulous attention to detail. We work closely with you to bring your ideas to life, tailoring our services to your preferences and exceeding your expectations. Our experienced team, backed by extensive industry knowledge and growing support system, manages every aspect of your event with professionalism and grace.
